# Design Partner Program Plan

## Target Profiles
- AI-first B2B startups with live FDE teams (3–10 members).
- Selling bespoke AI agents/models with complex onboarding (healthcare, fintech, enterprise SaaS).
- Willing to co-build workflows and provide weekly feedback.

## Recruitment Pipeline
1. Tap founder/FDE networks, investor portfolio, and LinkedIn outreach.
2. Offer incentive: dedicated workspace, roadmap influence, discounted pricing for 1 year.
3. Qualify via questionnaire (team size, current tooling, pain severity).

## Engagement Model
- **Week 0**: Alignment call, success metrics, data access checklist.
- **Week 1–2**: Onboard workspaces, migrate 1–2 live integrations.
- **Week 3+**: Weekly cadence (30 mins) to review feedback, deliver enhancements.
- Dedicated Slack channel per partner + shared notion doc for action items.

## Success Metrics
- ≥3 partners active within 4 weeks.
- Weekly active FDEs per org ≥70%.
- At least 2 critical feature requests per month funneled into roadmap.
- NPS ≥ 40 after 6 weeks.

## Feedback Loop
- Instrument product usage; share dashboards with partners.
- Run monthly retrospective summarizing wins, gaps, next bets.
- Publish anonymized case studies to support broader GTM.

## Expansion Path
- After MVP validation, introduce paid pilot tier w/ premium support.
- Build reference board of top FDE leaders for advisory council.
